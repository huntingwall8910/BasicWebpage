Ingredients to make pancakes:

  • Mix
  • 1 egg
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar

How to make the pancakes

  1. Add the mix
  2. Add the milk
  3. Add the egg
  4. Add the vanilla extract
  5. Stir it until there is no pores
  6. Add the butter
  7. Pour the desired amount into pan and wait about a minute
  8. Flip them over, wait a few more seconds, and Enjoy!!!!!
